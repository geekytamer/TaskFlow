# TaskFlow Usability Review — Backlog

Generated from a 13-role usability audit (one agent per job role) + synthesis. Average usability **5.5/10** across roles. Each item is checkbox-tracked.

> Note: the audit ran against the codebase right after Proposals was removed, so its "wire up the Proposals/Quotations page" suggestion is intentionally NOT included here (quotations, if wanted, should be a new multi-line quote feature, not the old page).

## Biggest risks (do first)

- [ ] DATA INTEGRITY — Issued invoices are silently mutable and desync from the ledger: a Sent invoice's amounts can be edited with no activity event while its idempotent journal stays frozen (postInvoiceJournal returns early), so the trial balance and the source document silently disagree with nothing flagging it. Unpaid invoices can also be hard-deleted along with their journals, leaving no audit footprint.
- [ ] COMPLIANCE — VAT is collected but never booked and there is no VAT report: revenue/P&L are overstated by the tax amount, account 2200 stays empty, and no output/input/net-payable figure can be produced. The product cannot serve a VAT-registered business or pass a tax audit.
- [ ] COMPLIANCE/INTEGRITY — Standalone expenses never reach the general ledger (createExpense writes only to the expenses table), so P&L, trial balance, and the 'Net Profit' KPI systematically overstate profit whenever the Expenses tab is used.
- [ ] DATA INTEGRITY — Lot/FEFO corruption: inter-warehouse transfers move balances but not lot locations, and negative stock adjustments never draw down lots, so expiry tracking breaks at destinations and phantom never-expiring lot quantities accumulate after every cycle count or write-off.
- [ ] AUDIT/CONTROL — The audit trail has major blind spots (no events for journals, payments, credit notes, chart-of-accounts edits, invoice amount edits, or period lock/unlock) and the period close is a soft, single editable date any Admin/Manager can roll backward with no reason, confirmation, or log—so the financial close is not tamper-evident.
- [ ] ACCESS CONTROL / LEAST-PRIVILEGE — No Procurement or Storekeeper roles: buyers must be granted Accountant/Manager (exposing full finance) and warehouse-floor staff are locked out of all inventory endpoints, while super-admins are structurally redirected out of the tenant settings they own. Several roles can only function by being over-privileged.
- [ ] SECURITY — Credential lifecycle is weak: no password reset of any kind (lost temp password = delete-and-recreate), temporary passwords are 8-char lowercase from a non-cryptographic Math.random() generated client-side and emailed in plaintext, no force-change-on-first-login, and no UI to create a backup super-admin (single point of failure).
- [ ] FINANCIAL CORRECTNESS — 'Multi-currency' is cosmetic (no exchange rates); any non-base-currency invoice is summed 1:1 into local totals, silently corrupting AR, aging, and the trial balance.
- [ ] MISSING CORE CAPABILITY — Whole job-defining workflows are absent at the UI: quotations (Sales), requisitions and true 3-way match (Procurement), per-customer aged statements and invoice delivery (Collections), and a real cycle-count workflow (Inventory).

## Top improvements (value-ordered: impact / effort)

### 1. Wire up already-built backend features to the UI (Quotations/Proposals, Purchase Requisitions, Credit Notes register)  _(impact: high · effort: low)_
- [ ] Three high-severity, workflow-blocking gaps share one root cause: the backend exists but the screen/route/call is missing. ProposalsPage just needs a route + nav entry; requisitions need a tab + 'Convert to PO' action; getCreditNotes needs a register tab. Near-zero build cost for very high relief because the hard part is done.
  - Roles affected: Sales Representative (Employee), Procurement / Purchasing Officer, Accounts Receivable / Collections Clerk, Compliance Officer / Auditor

### 2. Make VAT a first-class field and post it correctly (split tax to account 2200), then add a VAT return report  _(impact: high · effort: high)_
- [ ] The single biggest correctness/compliance blocker. Add a tax control to invoice creation, split postInvoiceJournal so tax credits 2200 Sales Tax Payable instead of dumping everything into Revenue, and add a tax dataset/report. Without this the ledger materially overstates revenue and no VAT-registered business can file or be audited.
  - Roles affected: Finance Manager / Accountant, Compliance Officer / Auditor, Accounts Receivable / Collections Clerk, CEO / Company Owner

### 3. Add real invoice delivery (email/share the existing PDF, stamp sentAt, log a delivery event)  _(impact: high · effort: medium)_
- [ ] 'Sent' is currently a no-op label and there is no way to get an invoice to a customer from inside the app, forcing manual download-and-attach for every invoice. The public-link pattern (publicDeliveryUrl) and email plumbing already exist—extend them to invoices with a 'Send to client' button. Makes 'Sent' trustworthy and closes the collections loop.
  - Roles affected: Accounts Receivable / Collections Clerk, Customer Success / Account Manager, Finance Manager / Accountant

### 4. Enforce document/ledger integrity: make issued invoices immutable (adjust via reversal), reverse rather than hard-delete journals, and persist Overdue status  _(impact: high · effort: medium)_
- [ ] Today a Sent invoice's amounts can be edited silently while its posted journal stays frozen, desyncing the trial balance from source documents with no audit trace; deleting an invoice hard-deletes its journals. Block financial edits to non-Draft invoices, route corrections through credit notes/reversing entries, and have the existing overdue sweep persist status so the filter agrees with the badge and aging.
  - Roles affected: Compliance Officer / Auditor, Finance Manager / Accountant, Accounts Receivable / Collections Clerk

### 5. Fix the lot/FEFO correctness bugs: move lots on transfer and draw down lots on negative adjustments  _(impact: high · effort: medium)_
- [ ] The headline batch/lot/expiry feature is silently corrupted by two backend gaps—createInventoryTransfer moves balances but not inventory_lots.location (breaking expiry/FEFO at the destination), and createInventoryAdjustment never calls drawDownLotsFEFO (leaving phantom, never-expiring lot quantities after every cycle count or write-off). Directly undermines the traceability the warehouse roles depend on.
  - Roles affected: Inventory / Warehouse Manager, Storekeeper / Warehouse Floor Staff, Compliance Officer / Auditor

### 6. Introduce dedicated/granular roles (Procurement, Storekeeper/Warehouse) and grant them their endpoints + nav  _(impact: high · effort: medium)_
- [ ] Procurement officers must be made Accountant/Manager and warehouse-floor staff (realistically Employees) are locked out of every inventory mutation endpoint and menu—both violate least-privilege and make the system unusable for those roles without over-privileging. Add roles to the enum, sidebar, and requireCompanyRoles on the relevant routes.
  - Roles affected: Procurement / Purchasing Officer, Storekeeper / Warehouse Floor Staff, IT / System Administrator (Super Admin)

### 7. Close the audit-trail blind spots and harden the period close  _(impact: high · effort: medium)_
- [ ] Manual journals, payments, credit notes, chart-of-accounts edits, invoice amount edits, and—critically—period lock/unlock emit no activity events, and the lock date can be rolled backward by any Admin/Manager with no reason or trail. Emit events (with structured old/new values) and require a reason + confirmation on unlock. Indefensible in an audit today.
  - Roles affected: Compliance Officer / Auditor, Finance Manager / Accountant, IT / System Administrator (Super Admin)

### 8. Make editable payment terms / due dates first-class on invoices and clients  _(impact: medium · effort: low)_
- [ ] Hardcoded Net-30 silently mis-ages every non-Net-30 customer, corrupting the aging report Finance and Collections live by. Add an editable issue/due date and a paymentTermsDays field on the Client that pre-fills it. Modest effort, fixes a root cause feeding several reports.
  - Roles affected: Finance Manager / Accountant, Accounts Receivable / Collections Clerk, Customer Success / Account Manager

### 9. Connect the dead-end lists and 360 views (clickable rows, drill-through, in-context actions)  _(impact: medium · effort: medium)_
- [ ] Make /clients, suppliers, and Kanban cards open their records; deep-link 360 tables to client-filtered views instead of whole-module pages; bring the activity feed + log/call/WhatsApp actions onto the contact 360. Removes constant cross-module hunting for several roles—mostly navigation/wiring, no new data.
  - Roles affected: Customer Success / Account Manager, Project Manager, Procurement / Purchasing Officer, Sales Representative (Employee)

### 10. Surface profitability and add time filtering/period deltas to executive reporting  _(impact: medium · effort: medium)_
- [ ] The owner's landing dashboard leads with tasks/receivables and buries net profit/revenue (already computed in finance-overview); the management report has no date range and no period-over-period deltas. Promote profit to the headline and add from/to controls so the board pack answers 'are we making money?' and 'vs last quarter?'.
  - Roles affected: CEO / Company Owner, Finance Manager / Accountant

## Cross-cutting themes

- BUILT BUT NOT WIRED: Fully-implemented backend capabilities have no UI route or are never called by any component. Hits Sales Rep (ProposalsPage/quotations unrouted), Procurement (purchase_requisitions + convertRequisitionToPurchaseOrder have zero frontend references), and AR/Collections (getCreditNotes implemented but rendered nowhere). The single highest-leverage class of fix—near-zero build cost, high relief.
- CAPTURED BUT IGNORED: Input fields are stored but silently dropped downstream, breaking user trust. VAT taxRate captured but never posted (Finance, Compliance, AR); billable-expense flag never reaches an invoice (Marketing, Finance); influencer agreedRate never produces a payment (Marketing); creditLimit decorative/unenforced (AR); reorderPoint global not per-warehouse (Inventory). The UI promises workflows the system doesn't execute.
- VAT/TAX IS NON-FUNCTIONAL: postInvoiceJournal credits the full gross total to Revenue 4000 and never touches account 2200 Sales Tax Payable; no VAT/return report exists anywhere. Independently flagged as high-severity by Finance Manager, Compliance Officer/Auditor, and AR Clerk—overstates revenue, makes filing impossible, makes the books wrong for any VAT-registered entity.
- MISSING/INADEQUATE ROLE MODEL & LEAST-PRIVILEGE: Roles are a fixed enum of four (Admin/Manager/Employee/Accountant). No Procurement role (buyers must become Accountant/Manager), no Storekeeper role (floor staff locked out of all inventory endpoints), no Collections role, and super-admins are structurally redirected out of tenant settings. Multiple roles must be over-privileged just to do their job.
- 'SENT' / DOCUMENT DELIVERY IS A NO-OP: There is no way to email or share an invoice from the app (download/print/PDF only), despite a working public-link pattern already existing for delivery notes. 'Sent' is a label that doesn't even stamp sentAt. Hits AR Clerk and Customer Success directly; PO documents are similarly unprintable for Procurement.
- HARDCODED NET-30 / NO PAYMENT TERMS: Invoice due date is hardcoded to issue + 30 days with no editable terms, which directly corrupts the aging buckets that Finance and AR/Collections depend on. Flagged by Finance Manager, AR Clerk, and PM (same hardcode in task-created invoices).
- SINGLE-LINE / NO-MULTI-LINE FORMS: Proposals support one line item (Sales), manual journal entries are capped at one debit + one credit (Finance), and PO-line receiving allows only one lot per line (Storekeeper). Core records can't represent realistic multi-line reality.
- DEAD-END LISTS & MISSING DRILL-THROUGH: Rows that should open a record don't—/clients rows (Customer Success), suppliers rows (Procurement), Kanban cards (PM). And generated documents (campaign invoices/bills, finance alerts) lack links back to the source. Constant cross-module hunting.
- RAW window.confirm/window.prompt FOR DESTRUCTIVE/INPUT ACTIONS: Inconsistent with the app's nicer useConfirm dialog; appears in project delete (PM), warehouse delete (Inventory), PO rejection reason (Procurement/Storekeeper), and company delete (CEO). Easy mis-fire on irreversible actions.
- THIN/UNFILTERABLE LISTS & LEDGERS: Hard caps with no search/filter/pagination recur—10-row stock-movement teaser (Inventory), 100-row activity log with no date/action filter (Compliance), no filters on HR tables, single status filter on task table (PM), 200-row admin audit log (IT). The audit/triage backbone is barely usable at scale.
- STRONG, GENUINELY-ENFORCED FOUNDATIONS (positive): Server-side RBAC (not just UI hiding), idempotent double-entry posting derived from journal_lines, accounting-period locking via assertOpenFinancialDate, server-side FEFO, and consistent Arabic/RTL with logical properties—praised across nearly every role.
- ROLES DISAGREE ON DRAG-AND-DROP: The PM praises smooth Kanban drag-and-drop on tasks while the Sales Rep finds the CRM pipeline has a misleading 'Drop here' label but no actual drag. Same affordance, opposite reality across two modules—an inconsistency to reconcile.

## Full per-role improvement lists

### Sales Representative — 6/10
- [ ] **Wire up the Proposals/Quotations page for sales reps** _(impact: high)_ — ProposalsPage and its Employee-scoped backend already exist; add an /crm/proposals route and a sidebar nav entry (roles including Employee) so reps can actually create, send, and track quotes. This closes the single biggest gap in my daily workflow at near-zero build cost since the component is done.
- [ ] **Give reps a scoped path to create a sales order from a won deal** _(impact: high)_ — Today Employee is blocked from sales-orders at both route and API. Either allow Employee to create Draft sales orders for their own won opportunities, or add a 'Request sales order' handoff that notifies a manager. Without this, the 'quote-to-order' half of my job lives entirely outside my reach.
- [ ] **Add real drag-and-drop to the Kanban (or relabel the affordance)** _(impact: medium)_ — The board shows a 'Drop here' placeholder but cards can't be dragged; moves require per-stage chevron clicks. Adding dnd-kit drag-to-stage would match the mental model and speed up daily pipeline grooming; at minimum, remove the misleading 'Drop here' text.
- [ ] **Support multiple line items (and inventory-item picker) in proposals** _(impact: medium)_ — Real quotes have several lines; the current single-row form forces me to undersell or quote outside the system. Reusing the multi-row item editor already present in sales-page.tsx would make proposals usable for actual deals.
- [ ] **Surface quote/order context inside the opportunity and contact 360** _(impact: medium)_ — Add a 'Quotes' section and a 'Create quote' action to the opportunity edit dialog, and a deal->order link on the contact 360. This removes cross-module hunting and makes the lead->deal->quote->order chain navigable from one place.
- [ ] **Make lead/contact ownership behavior consistent and explicit** _(impact: low)_ — Opportunities are owner-isolated for Employees but contacts default to Public/shared, so reps see each other's leads. Offer an optional 'my leads only' filter (or default new leads to the creating rep) so my pipeline and my contact book follow the same privacy rules.

### Finance Manager / Accountant — 5/10
- [ ] **Make VAT a first-class field on invoices and post it to 2200** _(impact: high)_ — Add a tax-rate/tax-amount control (per line or per invoice) to create-invoice-sheet.tsx, surface tax on the printed document, and split postInvoiceJournal so the tax portion credits the existing 2200 Sales Tax Payable account instead of dumping everything into Revenue. This is the single biggest blocker to using TaskFlow for a VAT-registered entity and to producing a correct VAT return.
- [ ] **Post standalone expenses to the general ledger** _(impact: high)_ — Have createExpense write a journal entry (DR chosen expense account / CR Cash or AP) and add an account selector to the Record Expense dialog. Today expenses silently never reach P&L or the trial balance, so net profit and the financial statements are wrong whenever the Expenses tab is used.
- [ ] **Add genuine multi-currency with exchange rates** _(impact: high)_ — Capture an exchange rate at invoice/bill/payment time, store both transactional and base-currency amounts, and convert before summing in overview/aging/reports. Without this, any non-base-currency transaction corrupts AR/AP totals and the trial balance — the current currency picker gives a false sense of capability.
- [ ] **Allow unlimited balanced lines in the manual journal entry dialog** _(impact: high)_ — Replace the fixed two-line form in journal-table.tsx with an add/remove-line grid that validates total debits = total credits. Multi-line journals (depreciation, payroll, accruals) are core accountant work and currently impossible.
- [ ] **Expose editable issue date and payment terms / due date on invoices** _(impact: medium)_ — Replace the hardcoded +30-day due date with a terms selector and editable issue/due dates (creation and edit). This directly improves the accuracy of the aging buckets the role depends on for collections.
- [ ] **Add detailed AR/AP aging drill-down and a balance sheet** _(impact: medium)_ — Let the overview aging buckets drill into the underlying invoices/bills, and add balance-sheet and (ideally) cash-flow reports alongside trial balance and P&L. These are standard period-end deliverables an accountant must hand to management/auditors.
- [ ] **Make bulk 'mark as paid' selective and safer** _(impact: low)_ — Require row selection (checkboxes) before bulk status changes and avoid a blanket 'all Sent -> Paid'. Recognizing payment without an actual receipt is a control risk for the finance role.

### Inventory / Warehouse Manager — 6/10

Drawing the transferred qty FEFO from source lots and re-creating/moving lot quantities at the destination keeps expiry traceability intact across warehouses — core to my job.
- [ ] **Draw down lots on negative adjustments (and depletion via FEFO)** _(impact: high)_ — createInventoryAdjustment must call the same FEFO drawdown as issues for negative quantityChange. Without it, every cycle-count correction or damage write-off leaves phantom lot quantities, so lot totals exceed on-hand and expired lots never clear. This directly corrupts the expiry data my role depends on.
- [ ] **Add a real cycle-count workflow (count session with variance)** _(impact: high)_ — Provide a per-warehouse count sheet where I enter counted quantities and the system computes variance and posts adjustments as one auditable count session. Replaces the current one-item-at-a-time signed-delta hack and gives a defensible audit trail — the single biggest day-to-day gap for a warehouse manager.
- [ ] **Per-warehouse reorder points and location-aware low-stock alerts** _(impact: medium)_ — Move reorder thresholds (or add a min level) to the location-balance level so a depleted warehouse triggers a signal even when total on-hand looks healthy. Multi-warehouse replenishment is invisible today.
- [ ] **One-click 'Create PO' from low-stock items, prefilled with supplier and suggested qty** _(impact: medium)_ — Close the loop between the low-stock signal and procurement. The system already knows preferred supplier, reorder point and on-hand; a Reorder button that opens a draft PO removes a manual, context-losing detour to Purchases.
- [ ] **PO-driven receiving with lot capture at the warehouse level** _(impact: medium)_ — Let me receive an open PO line-by-line into a warehouse, capturing lot/expiry per line, instead of opening each item's Lots dialog and retyping. Speeds up the most frequent inbound task and ties receipts to POs for reconciliation.
- [ ] **Show per-location available quantity inside Issue/Transfer/Adjust dialogs** _(impact: medium)_ — Display the selected warehouse's current balance (and active lot breakdown) next to the quantity field so I never discover an over-draw only via a failed save. Small change, removes guesswork on every movement.
- [ ] **Full, filterable stock-movement ledger** _(impact: medium)_ — Replace the 10-row company-wide teaser with a searchable/paginated ledger filterable by item, warehouse, type and date range (and reachable from the warehouse detail page). Stock movements are the audit backbone of the role and are currently barely usable.
- [ ] **Lot lifecycle actions: edit expiry/lot no, quarantine, recall, manual deplete** _(impact: medium)_ — Add edit and status controls in InventoryLotsDialog. Wrong expiry dates and recalls are routine, and right now a mistyped expiry is permanent and a recall is impossible without a fake negative adjustment.
